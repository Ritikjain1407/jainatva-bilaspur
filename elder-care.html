Pillar of Service · Elderly Care Support

Senior Citizens Support

Spreading warmth, joy, and emotional companionship to senior citizens in old-age homes across Bilaspur city.

Companionship & Care

Comforting Our Elders

The elderly population in old-age homes often faces deep isolation, seeking emotional warmth and simple human connection. At Jainatva, we believe in honoring their wisdom and bringing laughter back into their lives.

Our volunteers conduct regular monthly visits, hosting interactive storytelling sessions, singing traditional songs, distributing daily essentials, and celebrating major festivals together as one big family.

Medical Camps: Facilitating regular checkups, health tracking, and securing monthly medication logs.
Dignity & Respect: Supporting old-age facilities with infrastructure needs like blankets, clean linen, and sanitation supplies.

Focus Areas

  • Active Listening

    Spending hours hearing their life stories, validating their emotions, and providing deep psychological comfort.

  • Health & Prescription Aid

    Bridging the medical gap by sourcing life-saving medications, spectacles, and physical support gear.

  • Festive & Cultural Evenings

    Organizing bhajans, sharing festive meals, and presenting local arts to recreate a lively home atmosphere.

Active Campaign

Cloth Donation Drive

At Jainatva Bilaspur, we believe that every act of kindness has the power to transform lives. Our Cloth Donation Drive was organized with the mission of collecting and distributing clean, usable clothing to individuals and families in need, ensuring that dignity and compassion reach those who need them most.

This initiative brought together passionate volunteers who dedicated their time and efforts to collect, sort, and distribute clothing while encouraging the community to contribute towards a meaningful cause. More than just donating clothes, this drive promoted the values of empathy, sustainability, and social responsibility.

Through collective action, we strive to build a society where resources are shared with compassion, waste is reduced, and every individual has access to basic necessities. Every contribution, no matter how small, helps create a lasting impact and inspires others to serve humanity.
