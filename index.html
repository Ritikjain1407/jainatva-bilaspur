What We Do

Our Core Service Pillars

Jainatva Bilaspur operates across several sectors of community welfare, driven by volunteer energy and the philosophy of Ahimsa (non-violence) and Compassion.

Food Distribution

Regular langar drives, food packets for the needy, and nutrition support for underprivileged families across Bilaspur city and surrounding areas.

Read More

Education Support

Free tuition camps, stationery drives, and scholarship assistance for children from economically weaker backgrounds to help them achieve their potential.

Read More

Social Awareness

Campaigns on health, hygiene, girl education, anti-addiction, voter awareness, and sustainable living — reaching thousands through events and social media.
