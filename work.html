Core Activities

Eight Pillars of Service

The foundations of our voluntary drives conducted across Bilaspur and its neighboring zones.

Food Distribution

Regular langar drives, food packets for the needy, and nutrition support for underprivileged families across Bilaspur city and surrounding areas.

Drives: Weekly (Sundays) Impact: 4,000+ meals served

Education Support

Free tuition camps, stationery drives, and scholarship assistance for children from economically weaker backgrounds to help them achieve their potential.

Drives: Bi-weekly tuition + School kits Impact: 800+ children helped

Elderly Care

Visiting old-age homes, providing medicines, organizing cultural programs, and offering emotional support to senior citizens who need companionship.

Drives: Monthly visits Impact: 3 old-age homes supported

Social Awareness

Campaigns on health, hygiene, girl education, anti-addiction, voter awareness, and sustainable living — reaching thousands through events and social media.

Drives: Seminars + street plays Impact: 5,000+ citizens aware
