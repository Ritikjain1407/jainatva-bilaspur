About Jainatva Bilaspur

Rooted in compassion, powered by youth. Discover our history, core values, and vision for the future.

Our Story & Who We Are

Jainatva Bilaspur was born in 2019 from a simple belief: that every act of service, no matter how small, creates ripples of change. Founded by the passionate youth of Bilaspur, we unite individuals from all walks of life under the banner of Jain principles — compassion, non-violence, and selfless service.

Our name embodies our purpose — "Jainatva" meaning the essence of Jain values. We are not just an organization; we are a movement rooted in doing good, serving the forgotten, and building a more compassionate Chhattisgarh.

We operate transparently, welcoming every young citizen who wishes to volunteer their energy and skills. Together, we serve to inspire the community toward collaborative social change.

Our Vision

A society where compassion drives action and every individual is empowered to serve and protect all forms of life.

Our Mission

Mobilize and empower youth to deliver high-impact community programs in education, environment responsibility, healthcare, and stray animal welfare.

Why We Exist

To bridge the gaps in society, providing a reliable platform for youths to practice active service, non-violence (Ahimsa), and social stewardship right here in Bilaspur.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every action, every project, and every act of service we undertake in Bilaspur.

Ahimsa

अहिंसा

Non-violence in thought, word, and deed — toward all living beings, forming the basis of all animal feeding and environmental care campaigns.

Compassion

करुणा

Deep empathy for every person, animal, and life form around us, turning concern into actual helpful service on the ground.

Service

सेवा

Selfless giving of time, effort, and resources without expectation of return, purely for the upliftment of the needy.

Unity

एकता

Strength through togetherness, welcoming volunteers of all backgrounds, skills, and perspectives to serve as one family.

Integrity & Transparency

पारदर्शिता

Honesty in all reporting. We share our campaign outcomes, volunteers' attendance, and audit reports openly with our community.

Youth Leadership

युवा नेतृत्व

Empowering young minds to plan, coordinate, and lead social drives, building future-ready community leaders.

Our Roadmap

Organization Objectives & Future Goals

We aim to create a systematic, scalable model of local charity. Our goals are divided between day-to-day welfare campaigns and long-term infrastructure to support the needy in Bilaspur.

Zero Hunger Zones

Expand langar coverage to reach 5 fixed locations in Bilaspur every single Sunday.

Urban Afforestation Projects

Transition from simple planting drives to 3-year sapling nursing programs to guarantee survival.

Digital Seva Center

Open a free IT training center to help underprivileged kids learn foundational office tools and programming.
